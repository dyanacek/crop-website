The Seattle CROP Hunger Walk supports the international anti-hunger work of CWS and the following local agencies:

All Saints' Episcopal Food Bank
City Fruit
Mary's Place
North Helpline
West Seattle Food Bank
White Center Food Bank

Learn more about the how the Seattle CROP Hunger Walk changes lives around the globe by joining us for dinner and a special Seattle-area screening of Visionaries featuring the work of CWS, supported by the Seattle CROP Hunger Walk!

CWS has been featured in two episodes of the award-winning PBS documentary series Visionaries, which profiles the work of nonprofit organizations making a difference around the world. Hosted by Law & Order star Sam Waterston, Visionaries has been covering stories of hope and good work since 1995.

Join us Monday, September 16, 2013, at 6 pm at Gethsemane Lutheran Church (911 Stewart Street in Seattle). Please RSVP to lcurkendall@cwsglobal.org by September 9th.
