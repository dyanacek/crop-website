Location: Columbia Lakewood Community Church (5005 Ferdinand St, Seattle)
Registration: 12:30 p.m., Walk: 1 p.m.

This route is a 3-mile loop that begins and ends at Columbia Lakewood Community Church. From the church, head down to beautiful Lake Washington Boulevard, over to Seward Park, and back up through the neighborhood to the church. There will also be a shorter, one-mile route called the Golden Mile for those who would prefer a shorter Walk. Join us for lunch and fresh cider after the Walk at Columbia Lakewood Community Church!
