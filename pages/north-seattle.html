Location: Green Lake United Methodist Church (6415 1st Ave N, Seattle)
Registration: 12:30 p.m. Walk: 1:00 p.m.

From Green Lake UMC the route will head down the street to the path around beautiful Green Lake. The route is a paved 2.8 mile loop that is fully wheelchair and stroller accessible. Enjoy the turtles, ducks and on a clear day the views of Mount Rainier, the Cascades, and downtown Seattle. Again this year we will be lead by the bagpiper!
