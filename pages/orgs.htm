ORGANIZATIONS BEING SUPPORTED

The Seattle CROP Hunger Walk is a fundraiser organized under the auspices of Church World Service, and all donations are fully tax deductible. This fundraiser is unique in that it recognizes both the hunger in our own community as well as in the wider world. 25% of all funds raised are earmarked for local area Food Banks. 75% of the monies raised are designated for use in the wider world. For more information, please visit www.cropwalk.org.

Representatives from Northwest Harvest, the West Seattle Food Bank, and the White Center Food Bank will also be attending the Walk to answer any questions you may have regarding the current needs of local area food banks.

Church World Service does allow donors to designate an alternate recipient of the monies. For a partial listing of approved alternate recipients please visit the CWS web site. For a more extensive list, please contact the Seattle CROP Hunger Walk Organizing Committee by e-mail at SeattleCROPHungerWalk@gmail.com.
